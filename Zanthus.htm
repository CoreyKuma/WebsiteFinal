ZANTHUS

SPEAKER OF THE DEEP

Information

"Zanthus' contract was signed before he was born."

  • Zanthus uses the power of his patron to inflict fear into those who seek to harm his friends.
  • With the help of his patron "Sekolah", Zanthus uses the oceans deepest recesses to flood his foes terror.
  • Though he never asked for the contract with "Sekolah", he begrudgingly relies upon it to protect the ones most dearest to him.
